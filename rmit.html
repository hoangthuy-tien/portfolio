Student's Work

Studying at the university

Some works were done while I was studying digital media design (BP309) at RMIT University in 2019.


Video - Saigon In My Eyes

Ho Chi Minh City is also known as Saigon. My friends and I had to film many daily activities of citizens who are living in one of the important economy city of Vietnam. Starting a new day with an iced coffee is a Vietnamese habitant.

After a hard day with business and study, the dynamic nightlife in Ho Chi Minh City begins. The feeling of sitting on the sidewalk with cold beer bottles and colorful street lights makes life in this city even more sparkling at night.


Video - Motion Graphic

Seconds, Minutes, Hours and Days It is a song for children. The audio is used for teaching children how to count and the definition of time. The voice is clear and simple to hear, understand and remember although it is a bit of fasting. The audience is children so the melody of audio is delightful, fun, fast and active. Besides, bright colors are also considered.

There are two fonts: Patrick Hand (regular) and Schoolbell are chosen because the final render is for children so it cannot be the sharp or hard font. Some script or hand-written are more relevant thanks to friendliness and softness.

Packaging

My work was for AsPAC competition. It is a kind of rice which help people to save time and convenience. This products focused on everyone, especially are busy people who don't want to waste time for breakfast or lunch in the office.

Research and Analysis

Concept - The new packaging of Vietnamese instant rice is a new change about functions and forms of popular rice to help people have more convience, good health, and unpolluted environment. It solves the problems of instant rice at the moment. In addition, people will have a new experience by enjoying a new way through it.
Material - White kraft and kitchen foil
