Working at IDP Education

Duration: April 2019 - March 2020

I worked as an intern designer in IELTS Department for last course of the university. My responsibilies were to design publications of IELTS events: flyers, standees, carousel, and design ads on Facebook page. IDP offered me to continue as an official employer for Marketing Department of both IELTS and Study Abroad department.




IDP Education is an international education organisation offering student placement in foreign countries. IDP has more than 100 offices in 31 countries and 550 counsellors.

Publications

Printed materials are publications which I designed to run the advertising of IELTS Examinations in year.

Standee

They are standees which I designed for the IELTS campaigns. They include IELTS Exam on computer, voucher, promotion when registration, or guideline for candidates.

Flyer

Several flyers which I designed when I was working for IELTS department in IDP Education Vietnam. Their main purpose is to advertise for IELTS orientation, events or IELTS Exam with a partner of IDP such as Diplomatic Academy of Vietnam, high school, and language center.
